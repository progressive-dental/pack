In its advanced stages, gum disease attacks and breaks down the supporting structures of the mouth, making it the number one cause of tooth loss. Yet despite this risk, a very small percentage of patients actually seek treatment. We often hear patients who are debating treatment options express concern about traditional surgical procedures, hearing they can be time-consuming, painful and have long recovery times. As a minimally-invasive treatment alternative, we are proud to offer Regenerative Endoscopic Procedures (REP).

No Cutting or Stitches = Faster Recovery

Compared to traditional gum surgery,, REP is faster, offers quicker recovery times and is virtually painless. The simple and convenient minor surgical procedure is performed with a small fiber optic camera that the doctor places into the periodontal pocket to see beneath the gum line, clean the tooth roots and diagnose tooth decay or root erosion. With this treatment, you will still enjoy the same benefits of periodontal surgery to rid your mouth of gum disease and restore health to your smile. In addition, you can expect: Minimally-Invasive Procedure, More Comfortable Treatment and Heal More Quickly.
